The review Speed Racer

Alex Zayat, August 8th, 2024

Speed Racer is a highly underrated movie, that continues from a 1997 cartoon. Speed Racer is a fantasy movie, that is not meant to be compared to reality. The graphics are unbelievable, without knowing the release date you'd never know the movie was filmed in 2008. The actors put a lot of hard work and dedication, and watching the behind the scenes is even crazier. If you love fantasy vehicles, action scenes, and explosions this movie is for you!
